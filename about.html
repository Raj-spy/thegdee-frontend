Clear Shipping & Returns Policy

Processing Time: Orders are processed within 2-3 business days after payment confirmation.

Delivery Time: We deliver across India within 4-7 working days depending on your location.

Shipping Fees: Free shipping for all prepaid orders above ₹999. COD orders may have an additional charge.

Returns: You can request a return within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in the original packaging.

Refunds: Once we receive your return in our warehouse and verify its condition, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Exchanges: If you need a different size, we offer exchanges subject to product availability.
